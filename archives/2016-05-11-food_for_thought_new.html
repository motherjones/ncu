This Week's Main Dish
That Meal Kit Company That Sends You Dinner May Be Doomed

Let me guess: You don't cook as much as you wish you did. The whole rigmarole, from menu planning to navigating the store to chopping and cleaning everything up, seems like a time-sucking ordeal. Yet you've heard the messages: Home-cooked meals engender a healthier relationship with food and a richer family life.

But if you're like most Americans, achieving those kitchen aspirations will require some help. Left to our own devices, we now spend half our food dollars eating out. And even when we stay in, we often revert to takeout—only 50 percent of the main dishes served at home are cooked there.

Silicon Valley has just the thing for you.
