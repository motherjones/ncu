Here's Another Way That Oil Is Screwing the Environment
What do almonds, golf, fracking, and Kim Kardashian's lawn have in common? They've all been publicly shamed for their outsize water use during California's ongoing drought. But you likely haven't heard as much about one of the state's major water sucks: oil refineries, which are estimated to be the second biggest water user of non-ag businesses in the state (after golf). So just how much of California's precious water are refineries using?
