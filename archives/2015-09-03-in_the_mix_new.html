President Obama Is the Anti-Lame Duck

Quentin Tarantino really likes President Barack Obama. In a recent interview, he was asked to evaluate the president's time in office. "I think he’s fantastic," Tarantino said. "He’s my favorite president, hands down, of my lifetime. He’s been awesome this past year. Especially the rapid, one-after-another-after-another-after-another aspect of it. It’s almost like take no prisoners. His he-doesn’t-give-a-shit attitude has just been so cool. Everyone always talks about these lame-duck presidents. I’ve never seen anybody end with this kind of ending. All the people who supported him along the way that questioned this or that and the other? All of their questions are being answered now."
